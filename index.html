About Blues Clues

When Blue has a problem, Steve and the viewer, helps solve it by playing a game. They have to find clues left by Blue. Each clue narrows the problem down for the viewers. Steve writes the clues down and then, along with the viewer, thinks carefully to piece all the clues together and solve the problem. Steve always asks preschoolers questions and then waits for an answer because they can’t solve “Blue’s Clues” without the audience’s help. This show includes Math, Science, Literacy, arts, and problem solving!
